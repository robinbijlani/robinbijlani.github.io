  • Exotics database

    backend development

    This online database was created for the University of Florida and Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission to input, share, and query data regarding their collected invasive species. Records include intake information, necropsy results, telemetry field data, etc. and maps can be generated from custom queries.

  • EIRAMP Tablet app

    mobile app development

    The University of Florida's Everglades Invasive Reptile and Amphibian Monitoring Program (EIRAMP) involves road cruising standard routes and surveying native and nonnatiive reptiles and amphibians. The EIRAMP tablet app facilitates data recording and validates the entered data, greatly reducing human error as well as data entry and proofing time.
